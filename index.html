Short answers to questions you have about

COVIDeo

No media or film experience?

You do not need to have media or film experience to participate! We will hold "pre-hack" huddles in the weeks before the event, where we will go over basic media creation, editing, and collaboration skills and software. We will also gather information about participants' experience in a follow-up email to be sure we spread media and software skills evenly across teams.

Other skills needed?

You don't need any special skills to participate; just a laptop with a webcam and your patience & enthusiasm! We want this to be a learning opportunity, so if you don't have any film or media experience we will help you find your role on the team. You'd be surprised how important a cheerful face is when you're 30 hours into a project...

Already have a team or an idea?

COVIDeo is an opportunity to meet new people from across the world! We intend to place every person on a team of creatives they did not know before the event. This will help us all create new, diverse content and build new connections across borders. If your participation is contingent on working with someone you know, please reach out to us to discuss this possibility.

Only partially available?

We do not expect participants to work the full 48 hours (one of the benefits of working across time-zones!), but you can expect to spend most of your waking hours engaged with your team. Please only sign up for the event if you are able to contribute a significant amount of your time and energy to your team during the event's weekend.
